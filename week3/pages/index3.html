Incrideble Types

Neus Ortiz

Love this corporate identity designed by Gerard Marin for Spanish interior designer Neus Ortiz! The centrepiece is the three dimensional ‘N’, which is neatly textured using a variety of geometric patterns to create a dynamic branding system suitable for a range of different applications. When scaled and cropped as seen below the brand mark also functions very nicely as an abstract illustrative element. If you’re a fan I recommend checking out the rest of Marin’s excellent portfolio of work.
